The reason that I selected computer science major and dedicated to the internet industry, I don’t just want to become a person that witnesses how technology helps more people, but also I long to become a person that makes technology help us.

As a person who thrives in high-pressure and fast-paced situations, I strive for positive results through the application of my leadership skills and communication abilities.
